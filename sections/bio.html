Bio

Before coming to MSR, I obtained a PhD in computer science from MIT, where I worked on various topics in machine learning and natural language processing under the supervision of Tommi Jaakkola. I also hold BSc (Licenciatura) and MS degrees in mathematics from ITAM and Courant Institute (NYU), respectively. During the latter, I worked on semidefinite programming for domain adaptation under the supervision of Mehryar Mohri. Between Master's and PhD, I spent a year at IBM's T.J. Watson Research Center, working with Ken Church and others in the Speech Recognition Group.
